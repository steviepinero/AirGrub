Frequently Asked Questions

    What is AirGrub?

    We are a group of foodies with a network of farmers, homeowners, and fruit and vegetable purveyors in South Florida. Our goal is to support healthy eating habits while promoting sustainable agriculture.

    How do I use AirGrub?

    Please set up your account at AirGrub.com. You will need to provide us with your address and credit card information. Once you are registered, just log on to our webpage, make your selections, include payment information, and your produce will be delivered by a friendly drone, healthily armed with your fruits and veggies.

    How do I order?

    If you haven't yet set up an account at www.AirGrub.com, you will need to do so before placing your order. Once you are registered, just log on to the website, make your selections, verify payment information, and your produce will be delivered quickly.

    Where is my order?

    You can track your order in real time at www.AirGrub.com. Our drones are equipped with GPS.

    Can I change my order after I place it?

    If you placed your order in the last five minutes, yes. If it has been more than five minutes, you will need to place a second order extra fee. Sorry!

    Is my personal information, include address and credit card information, secured?

    Yes, it is. Our propietary software secures your information.

    If I have a fruit tree in my yard, and the tree bears a lot of fruit, can I sell it through your website?

    Of course. Just call us at (305) 570-9768 and we'll provide you the details on how to join our network.
